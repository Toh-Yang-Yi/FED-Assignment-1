JungKook

Jungkook is a singer, songwriter, producer, and dancer. He is the main vocalist, center, lead dancer, and sub rapper of the band, BTS. In 2022, Jungkook officially started his career as a solo artist. Known as a musical prodigy and as one of the greatest vocalists in history, Jungkook’s record-breaking solo debut has earned him recognition as one of the biggest global superstars of all time.
